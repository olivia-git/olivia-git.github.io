About Crepe N'Go


What We Offer

Crepe N'Go offers delicious dining and takeout to Rochester, NY.

Crepe N' Go is a cornerstone in the Rochester community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.

Specialties

Chinese crepes are savory high protein and nutritious.

We make them to order with the fillings of your choice from our delicious fresh meats, Tofu and frsh lettuce, tomato, cilantro and scallions...sauces can be savory, salty, sweet, and spicy!


History

Established in 2016.

We are the newest business on Monroe Avenue in the business district of Oxford Square. Our restaurant specializes in Chinese street style Crepes for take-out and dine-in a casual atmosphere. All items are in To Go packaging.
